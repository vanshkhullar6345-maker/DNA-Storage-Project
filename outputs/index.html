About DNA Storage
Molecular-scale archival storage
DNA data storage converts digital information into DNA nucleotide sequences (A, T, G, and C). DNA offers extremely high storage density, durability for thousands of years, and low long-term energy consumption compared to conventional storage devices. This project demonstrates the workflow of DNA data encoding, digital archiving, and retrieval through software simulation.
